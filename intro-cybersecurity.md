##  What this room was about
This room introduced the fundamentals of cybersecurity and explained why protecting systems and data is important.

##  What I learned
- Cybersecurity helps protect systems, networks, and data from attacks
- There are different types of cyber threats like phishing, malware, and brute force attacks
- The CIA Triad:
  - Confidentiality → keeping data private
  - Integrity → ensuring data is not altered
  - Availability → ensuring systems are accessible
