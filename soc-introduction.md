# Introduction to SOC (Security Operations Center) - TryHackMe

## What this room is about
This room introduces the concept of a Security Operations Center (SOC) and explains how organizations monitor and respond to security threats.

## What I learned
- A SOC is responsible for monitoring, detecting, and responding to cyber threats
- SOC analysts work to protect systems and investigate suspicious activity
- There are different levels of SOC analysts (Level 1, Level 2, Level 3)
- SOC teams use tools like SIEM to monitor logs and detect threats

## Key Concepts
- Monitoring network and system activity
- Incident detection and response
- Log analysis and alert investigation
- Threat identification

## Key Takeaway
A SOC plays a critical role in cybersecurity by continuously monitoring systems and responding to potential threats to protect an organization.
