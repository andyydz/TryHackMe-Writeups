# CIA Triad Concepts

## Confidentiality
Confidentiality ensures that sensitive information is only accessible to authorized users.

### Examples
- Password protection
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA)
- Data encryption
- Access control policies

### Cybersecurity Relevance
Confidentiality helps prevent:
- Unauthorized access
- Data leaks
- Credential theft
- Sensitive information exposure

---

## Integrity
Integrity ensures that data remains accurate, trustworthy, and unmodified without authorization.

### Examples
- File hashing
- Digital signatures
- Access restrictions
- Change monitoring

### Cybersecurity Relevance
Integrity helps detect:
- Unauthorized modifications
- Data tampering
- Malware activity
- Corrupted files

---

## Availability
Availability ensures that systems, applications, and data remain accessible when needed.

### Examples
- Backup systems
- Redundancy
- Disaster recovery
- DDoS protection

### Cybersecurity Relevance
Availability helps organizations maintain:
- Business continuity
- System uptime
- Reliable access to services
- Operational stability
